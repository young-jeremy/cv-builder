Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best resume format?

The best resume format depends on your work experience and career situation. The most common formats are:

  • Chronological: Best for candidates with a consistent work history and career progression.
  • Functional: Ideal for those with employment gaps or career changers.
  • Combination: Perfect for highlighting both skills and work experience.

Our resume builder helps you choose the optimal format based on your specific situation.

How long should my resume be?

For most candidates, a one-page resume is ideal. However, if you have extensive relevant experience (typically more than 10 years), you might extend to two pages. Our builder helps you optimize your content to fit the appropriate length while keeping all essential information.

What should I include in my resume?

A complete resume should include:

  • Contact information
  • Professional summary or objective
  • Work experience
  • Education
  • Skills
  • Achievements and certifications

Optional sections might include languages, projects, volunteer experience, or interests. Our builder guides you through each section with expert tips.

Will my resume pass ATS systems?

Yes! All our templates are ATS-friendly and designed to pass through Applicant Tracking Systems. Our builder also provides keyword suggestions based on your target job to increase your chances of getting past automated screening.

Can I download my resume in different formats?

Absolutely! You can download your resume in multiple formats including PDF, Word (.docx), and plain text. Premium users also get access to additional formats and the ability to create unlimited resumes.
